About Scoop Deville

Scoop DeVille is a Philadelphia ice cream institution! Started in 1989, Scoop DeVille has provided Philadelphians, and tourists alike, with the most memorable ice cream experience in town! With our famous custom Blends, we are the only ice cream shop that let's you create your own flavor of soft serve on the spot! From bananas to bacon, we have hundreds of mix-ins, and the flavor combinations are endless!
